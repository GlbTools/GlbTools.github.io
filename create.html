Let’s Create Your Own GLB Site

Please log in with GitHub to start creating.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Fork Clone.Tools

Enter a unique name above (e.g., "rock.tools") and click "Fork Repo" to create your site.

Step 2: Check Fork Status

Status: Waiting to start...

Step 3: Configure Your Site

Use "My Portal" in the dropdown profile on your new website to configure it.

Step 4: Enable GitHub Pages

Go to your repo’s Settings > Pages, set the source to the main branch, and save.

Step 5: Upload Your GLBs

In "My Portal" on your new site, go to "Site Configuration" and set a repo for your GLB files under "GLB Repo Name" (e.g., "my-glb-repo"). Save the config, then use the "Upload Model" section to add your .glb files, along with optional .txt and .png files.

Step 6: Share Your Creation

Once set up, select "Go Public" in My Portal to allow GLB.TOOLS to find your website.
