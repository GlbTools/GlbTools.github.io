Let’s Create Your Own GLB Site

Please log in with GitHub to start creating.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Fork Clone.Tools

Enter a unique name above (e.g., "car-kit") and click "Fork Repo" to create your site.

Step 2: Check Fork Status

Status: Waiting to start...

Step 3: Create GLB Repo (Where we will save our .GLB Files)

Enter a name for your GLB storage repo and click "Create GLB Repo" to set it up.

Step 4: Check GLB Repo Status

Status: Waiting to start...

Step 5: Enable GitHub Pages

Click here to go to your new repo's GitHub Pages setup, set Branch to main and Save.

Your site will be live at: Checking... (may take a few minutes after enabling).

Step 6: Upload Your GLBs

Visit your portal and use the "Upload Model" section to add your .glb files, along with optional .txt and .png files.

Step 7: Share Your Creation

Visit your portal and select "Go Public" to allow GLB.TOOLS to find your website.
