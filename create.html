Let’s Create Your Own GLB Site

Please log in with GitHub to start creating.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Fork Clone.Tools

Visit Clone.Tools on GitHub and click "Fork" to create your own copy.

2. Name Your Site

Enter a name for your site above. Click "Create Repo" to set it up automatically, or manually rename your fork on GitHub.

3. Configure Your Site

Edit config.json in your repo with your site title, thumbnail path, and repo details. Upload a thumbnail.jpg (800x450px, max 100KB) to your repo root.

4. Enable GitHub Pages

Go to your repo’s Settings > Pages, set the source to the main branch, and save. Your site will be live at https://yourusername.github.io/your-repo-name.

5. Share Your Creation

Once set up, your site will appear on GLB.TOOLS automatically (if tagged with glbtools). Share it with the world!
